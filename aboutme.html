Valentina Del Viscio

When I was five years old, I spent my days drawing on Paint or sketching houses on Windows 98. Then, as a teenager, I enjoyed creating photo montages, designing flyers, billboards and sets for dance shows.

As I grew older, I joined the Architecture Faculty in Ferrara.

During my Erasmus year in Valencia, I delved into Visual Communication Design classes and found myself fully captivated by that field. There I realized it was the domain I truly enjoyed.

After completing my Master's degree in Architecture, I undertook another Master's degree in Digital Communication Design in Turin, where I found my true passion for UX/UI design.

Currently I work passionately as a UX/UI designer in a consulting company, where I enthusiastically embrace the chance to learn new concepts daily and gain a deeper understanding of user needs.
