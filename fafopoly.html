Faf-opoly

A similar-Monopoly centered around Ferrara and the Architecture Faculty

Project type
Personal
Collaborative

Tools
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Photoshop
Adobe InDesign

Duration
1 month

Prompt

"Faf" is the acronym for the Faculty of Architecture of Ferrara. To celebrate the graduation of some classmates, this game was devised, focused on the spaces shared together and especially on the people in the class.

Game board

Instead of the four iconic stations on the Monopoly board, they have been replaced with the four most important events in Ferrara:
"Il Palio", "Buskers Festival”, “Interno Verde” and the "Internazionale Festival."

The player who manages to acquire all four symbolizes the creative director in the game.

Instead of the utilities (electricity and water in the original game), two "parties" have been inserted, the recurring ones that the class attended.

Furthermore, instead of "Jail," there is "Quarantine," because the game is also set in the temporal context of 2021.

In the parking lot, bicycles are depicted because Ferrara is known as the city of bicycles.

Money

Even the money is customized for the city of Ferrara: various monuments have been featured and classified by "importance" on the banknotes.

For example, the smallest denomination of currency features a palace where an architectural survey exam was held together.

Cards

The Chance and Community Chest cards are personalized to the people in the class.

Each Chance or Community Chest card is tied to a real event or amusing anecdote experienced with the person in question.

A gift

As mentioned before, the game is a gift, and in its package, it also includes a rulebook along with a dedication.

The rulebook features the recurring texture in its entirety, which is the silhouette of all the people in the class.
