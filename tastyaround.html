Prompt

The project focused on creating a university campus in Sarajevo, with each team of three assigned a building of interest.

Among various faculties, a sports complex, and an auditorium, our team was assigned a building designated for study halls.

Concept

It houses a street on the ground and hosts glass bridges on the upper floor to recreate a suspended unique space.

Suggestion

We correlated each study type with an analysis of the furnishings used to create that particular study environment.

Each type of study requires different levels of concentration and/or interaction, as well as an available proper space.

The interior design begins with the research of the most suitable furniture for each request.

Building structure

Indoors, you'll find several areas categorized by study type, along with a recreational zone.

The exterior concrete covering evokes the pattern of crumpled paper.
